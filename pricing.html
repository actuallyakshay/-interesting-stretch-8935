Frequently asked questions

Below you’ll find answers to the questions we get asked the most about our subscriptions
Which subscription is right for me?

No two companies are the same and no one should pay for what they don't need. It's exactly why we offer different pricing plans for different businesses – whether you're a large company, small team or moonlighting freelancer.

Before choosing a subscription, you can trial Timely free for 14 days. During your trial, you have unlimited access to all of Timely’s features, so you can work out which features give you the most value.

What happens when my trial ends?

Once your trial period ends, everything you've tracked in Timely will be saved, so there's no need to rush into a decision. If you do choose to subscribe, everything—all your logged hours, projects, users, reports and memories—will be there as you left it.

If you don't know how much time you have left in your trial, head to Settings > Subscriptions and check your trial counter in the top-right corner.

Once your trial expires, you will need to purchase a subscription to continue using Timely. While you can still see the Memory history from your trial period even if you aren't subscribed, you won't be able to actually use any Timely functionality—like logging time or creating projects and reports—until you subscribe to a plan.

Where do I subscribe to Timely?

Your trial period has ended and you can't imagine going back to manual time tracking ever again. 💃💥 We know the feeling... Thankfully, it only takes a few seconds to find and subscribe to the right Timely plan for you.

To get started:
1. sign in to Timely
2. click the Settings icon on the left-hand navigation bar
3. choose Subscriptions and take it from there.  

If you have any questions, just reach out to us through support chat or email support@timelyapp.com and we’ll be right with you.

Do you offer discounts?

At Timely, we offer two types of discount:
1. Annual plan discount
Get up to a 23% discount by signing up for a yearly subscription instead of a monthly one. Don't let that money fly out the door; take advantage of this and save, save, save!

2. Student discounts
Send us a note in our support chat or email support@timelyapp.com and we'll help you out. Please note, you must provide a valid student ID and valid student email to sign up.

I’m missing features that were available during my trial. What can I do?

During the free trial, you can access all of Timely's features. However, they may not be included in the final plan you choose to subscribe to.  You can see a full breakdown of what is included in each subscription by clicking on “learn more” on our pricing page

If you realize you're missing something crucial after subscribing, reach out to our Success team who can explain our plans and any in-app "upgrade" messages you may have encountered.

None of these plans really fit my needs. Who can I talk to?

Drop us a line at sales@timelyapp.com, briefly answering these questions and we’ll be happy to help:

·  Who are you and what do you do? (Tell us about how awesome your company is and what you're up to!)
·  How many folks will be tracking time?
·  How did you hear about Timely?
·  What was your motivation for finding a time tracking tool?
