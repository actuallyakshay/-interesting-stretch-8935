Frequently asked questions

Below you’ll find answers to the questions we get asked the most about our subscriptions

Which subscription is right for me? No two companies are the same and no one should pay for what they don't need. It's exactly why we offer different pricing plans for different businesses – whether you're a large company, small team or moonlighting freelancer.

Before choosing a subscription, you can trial Timely free for 14 days. During your trial, you have unlimited access to all of Timely’s features, so you can work out which features give you the most value.
What happends when my trial ends? Once your trial period ends, everything you've tracked in Timely will be saved, so there's no need to rush into a decision. If you do choose to subscribe, everything—all your logged hours, projects, users, reports and memories—will be there as you left it.

If you don't know how much time you have left in your trial, head to Settings > Subscriptions and check your trial counter in the top-right corner.

Once your trial expires, you will need to purchase a subscription to continue using Timely. While you can still see the Memory history from your trial period even if you aren't subscribed, you won't be able to actually use any Timely functionality—like logging time or creating projects and reports—until you subscribe to a plan.
Where do I subscribe my Timely ? Your trial period has ended and you can't imagine going back to manual time tracking ever again. 💃💥 We know the feeling... Thankfully, it only takes a few seconds to find and subscribe to the right Timely plan for you.
  1. 1. sign in to Timely
  2. 2. click the Settings icon on the left-hand navigation bar
  3. 3. If you have any questions, just reach out to us through support chat or email support@timelyapp.com and
  4. 4. choose Subscriptions and take it from there.
To get started: we’ll be right with you.
